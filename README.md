**SmartBuild** is a proposed digital platform designed to modernize the construction industry in developing markets. ## ğŸ›‘ The Problem
The current construction landscape suffers from three critical inefficiencies:

1.  **Efficiency Gaps:** Approx. 35% of project timelines are delayed due to poor labor allocation and manual tracking.
2.  **Material Wastage:** Theft and mismanagement of inventory (Cement, Steel) lead to significant cost overruns.
3.  **Communication Silos:** severe disconnect between on-site foremen and off-site project managers.
